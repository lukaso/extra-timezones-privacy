Privacy Policy - Extra Time Zones for Google Calendar

Last updated: 14 July 2025

OVERVIEW

This extension adds unlimited additional time zones to Google Calendar without collecting personal data.

DATA COLLECTION

This extension does not automatically collect, store, or transmit any personal calendar data or user information.

PERMISSIONS USED

  • calendar.google.com access: Required to display additional time zones on Google Calendar interface
  • storage: Only used to save your timezone preferences locally on your device
  • activeTab: Required to open optional feedback forms when requested by user

OPTIONAL FEEDBACK

  • Feedback collection occurs only when you voluntarily click "Send Feedback"
  • Feedback is submitted through Google Forms and follows Google's privacy policies
  • You may provide feedback anonymously or include your email for responses
  • Feedback is used solely to improve the extension

NO TRACKING

We do not use analytics, tracking, monitoring, or data collection services.

LOCAL OPERATION

This extension operates entirely within your browser except for voluntary feedback submission.

DATA RETENTION

  • Timezone preferences are stored locally until extension is uninstalled
  • Feedback submissions follow Google Forms' data retention policies

CONTACT

For privacy questions, contact: lukaso+timezones@gmail.com

UPDATES

This policy may be updated to reflect extension changes.
